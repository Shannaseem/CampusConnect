Frequently Asked Questions

How long does approval take?

Admin approval usually takes 24-48 hours. You'll receive an email once approved.

Can I change my password?

Yes! You can change your password anytime from your profile settings in the dashboard.

How do I submit late assignments?

You can submit assignments even after the deadline. Your teacher will see it marked as late.

Do you support file uploads?

Yes! You can upload any type of file - documents, images, PDFs, code files, and more.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard SSL encryption and comply with GDPR standards.

Can I login with social media?

Yes! You can sign in with Google, GitHub, or Facebook for quick and easy access.
